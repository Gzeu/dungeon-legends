📜 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Navigate through 5 dungeon rooms, defeat the Ancient Dragon, and collect treasure! Level up your heroes to unlock powerful abilities.

⚔️ Heroes & Progression

  • 🛡️ Knight (Lv.1-5): Tank with protection abilities. Unlocks Paladin at Lv.3
  • 🧙 Wizard (Lv.1-5): Spell damage and combos. Unlocks Necromancer at Lv.3
  • 🗡️ Rogue (Lv.1-5): Stealth and treasure bonuses. Unlocks Assassin at Lv.3
  • ⛪ Cleric (Lv.1-5): Healing and support magic. Unlocks Druid at Lv.3

🎲 Game Modes

  • Multiplayer: 2-4 human players, pass-and-play
  • Single Player Coop: You + AI allies vs dungeon
  • Single Player vs AI: Competitive treasure hunt

✨ Magic System

  • Mana: Each spell costs mana, regenerates each turn
  • Spell Schools: Fire, Ice, Nature, Shadow, Light
  • Combos: Cast 2+ spells of same school for bonus effects
  • Equipment: Persistent items that enhance abilities

📚 Level System

  • XP Sources: Combat (10), Room clear (15), Dragon (50), Treasure (5), Combos (8)
  • Rewards: +1 Attribute Point and +1 Skill Point per level
  • Skill Trees: 3 paths per hero (Combat/Magic/Support branches)
  • Unlocks: New heroes, dungeons, and legendary equipment

🏰 Rooms

  1. Entrance Hall: Safe start, draw extra card
  2. Goblin Warren: Fight Goblin (2HP/1ATK), +1 treasure
  3. Trap Chamber: Discard 2 cards or take damage, +2 treasure
  4. Treasure Vault: Fight Orc (4HP/2ATK), +3 treasure
  5. Dragon's Lair: Ancient Dragon (10HP/3ATK) with Fire Breath pattern

🏆 Victory

Cooperative: All survive and defeat Dragon
Competitive: Most treasure when Dragon falls
Progression: Heroes keep XP and levels between games
