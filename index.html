🏰 Dungeon Legends

Dark Gothic Adventure

📜 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Navigate through 4 dungeon rooms, defeat the Dragon boss, and collect the most treasure!

⚔️ Heroes

  • 🛡️ Knight: +2 Defense, can protect others
  • 🧙 Wizard: +3 Spell damage, area effects
  • 🗡️ Rogue: +1 Treasure, bypass traps
  • ⛪ Cleric: +2 Healing, revive allies

🏰 Rooms

  1. Entrance Hall: Safe start, draw extra card
  2. Goblin Warren: Fight Goblin (2HP/1ATK), +1 treasure
  3. Trap Chamber: Discard 2 cards or take damage, +2 treasure
  4. Treasure Vault: Fight Orc (4HP/2ATK), +3 treasure
  5. Dragon's Lair: Boss fight! Dragon (10HP/3ATK)

🏆 Victory

Cooperative: All survive and defeat Dragon
Competitive: Most treasure when Dragon falls
